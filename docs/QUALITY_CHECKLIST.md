# Final Video Quality Checklist

Use this checklist before approving any Dein-Dein promotional video. Record
results and exceptions in
[templates/FINAL_CREATIVE_REVIEW.md](templates/FINAL_CREATIVE_REVIEW.md).

## Brief and Story

- [ ] The video promotes one feature and one primary audience benefit.
- [ ] The hook is readable and understandable within the first two seconds.
- [ ] The middle of the video shows real feature proof.
- [ ] The story remains understandable when muted.
- [ ] The video ends with one clear next step.
- [ ] The closing frame remains stable for at least `1.5 seconds`.

## Facts and Product Accuracy

- [ ] Every visible or spoken claim matches an approved trusted source.
- [ ] Product behavior, UI states, links, event details, and showtimes are real.
- [ ] Unknown or unconfirmed details are omitted or marked pending.
- [ ] The edit does not imply Dein-Dein is the organizer, cinema, or ticket
      seller unless the brief confirms it.
- [ ] Urgency, availability, ratings, popularity, and outcomes are not invented.

## Brand and Copy

- [ ] The visible brand name is `Dein-Dein`.
- [ ] `dein-dein.com` is used correctly as the website URL.
- [ ] The legacy name `Event Dein` does not appear.
- [ ] The official logo is intact, legible, and correctly spaced.
- [ ] Navy is dominant, gold is focused, and plum is restrained.
- [ ] Typography is readable on a phone.
- [ ] There is one approved primary CTA.
- [ ] `Book Now` is absent unless explicitly approved under the brand rules.

## Motion and Product Footage

- [ ] Every crop, pan, zoom, transition, and effect has a communication purpose.
- [ ] Important UI regions are large enough to read.
- [ ] The product footage settles long enough to understand.
- [ ] Motion does not alter or obscure factual details.
- [ ] There is no rapid flashing, excessive glitching, constant zooming, or
      unreadable kinetic type.
- [ ] The final logo remains still after its reveal.

## Captions and Accessibility

- [ ] All meaningful spoken content has accurate burned-in captions.
- [ ] Captions and key copy remain within platform-safe areas.
- [ ] Text has strong contrast and does not rely only on color.
- [ ] Text does not cover essential UI, faces, or critical imagery.
- [ ] Copy is concise, correctly spelled, and visible long enough to read.

## Privacy, Rights, and Safety

- [ ] No personal data, secrets, private tabs, notifications, bookmarks, or
      admin controls are visible.
- [ ] All music, sound effects, footage, imagery, logos, and fonts have confirmed
      usage rights.
- [ ] Third-party artwork and partner logos are used only as approved.
- [ ] The video contains no misleading edits, cultural stereotypes, or unsafe
      imagery.
- [ ] Required attribution and disclaimers are present.

## Audio

- [ ] Music and sound effects are rights-cleared.
- [ ] Audio supports the pacing without overpowering the message.
- [ ] Interaction sounds are restrained and meaningful.
- [ ] There are no accidental system sounds, notifications, clipping, or harsh
      level changes.

## Export and Review

- [ ] Resolution is `1080x1920` for the default vertical reel, or matches the
      approved exception.
- [ ] Frame rate and playback are stable.
- [ ] No unintended blank frames, rendering artifacts, or clipped elements
      appear.
- [ ] The hook, feature-proof frame, and CTA frame were reviewed as stills.
- [ ] The full export was reviewed with audio.
- [ ] The full export was reviewed muted.
- [ ] The complete export was reviewed on a phone-sized display.
- [ ] The approved export is stored and named according to
      [ASSET_MANAGEMENT.md](ASSET_MANAGEMENT.md).

## Approval Rule

Approve only when all applicable items pass. Document any non-applicable item
or approved exception with its owner and reason.
