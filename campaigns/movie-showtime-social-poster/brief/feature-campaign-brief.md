# Movie Showtime Social Poster Campaign Brief

## Approved Contract

- **Asset:** Reusable movie-showtime social poster
- **Format:** Fixed `1080x1350`, `4:5` PNG
- **Audience:** People looking for Indian-language movie showtimes in Germany
- **Inputs:** Required category and region; optional selected movie title
- **Primary CTA:** `Find showtimes`
- **Logo:** Official `dein-new-logo.png`, intact and always visible
- **Imagery:** Typography-only; no movie artwork or third-party imagery
- **Required disclaimer:** `Dein-Dein is an independent discovery platform. Confirm showtimes, availability, and booking terms with the official provider.`
- **Output policy:** CLI renders review PNGs under `working/renders/`; approval is a separate review decision

Events and event-specific poster behavior are outside this version.
